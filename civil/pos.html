Civil Engineering Program Objectives

Program Educational Objectives for Civil Engineering

  • To design safe, economical and environmentally viable and sustainable solutions for civil engineering problems with the knowledge acquired during the course learning.
  • To acknowledge the need for personal and professional adaptability.
  • To develop professionally in their careers, through continued development of technical, management and communication skills and leadership qualities.
  • To exhibit professional responsibility and an appreciation of societal, ethical, regulatory, and global issues concerned to engineering projects.

Program Specific Outcomes for Civil Engineering

  • Function as design consultants in construction industry for the design of civil engineering structures.
  • Provide sustainable solutions to the Civil Engineering Problems.
  • Provide an ability to function on multidisciplinary teams.
  • Provide an ability to communicate effectively.
  • To provide recognition of the need for, and an ability to engage in life-long learning.
  • Design a system, component or process to meet the constituencies needs within realistic constrains.
  • Function on multidisciplinary teams.
  • Understand professional and ethical responsibility.
