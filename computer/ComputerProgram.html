Program Outcomes for Computer Engineering

Engineering Fundamentals

Acquire knowledge base of mathematics, science, and engineering to identify, formulate, and solve Computer engineering problems.

Technical Competency

Building competency in the field of Computer Engineering on the basis of strong technical as well as problem-solving skills.

Leadership & Teamwork

Recognize the need of soft skills to develop leadership skills and teamwork in curricular as well as extracurricular areas for overall development.

Life-Long Learning

Recognize the need for, and have the preparation and ability to engage in independent and life-long learning in the broadest context of technological change.

Environment and Sustainability

Understand the influence of professional engineering solutions in social and environmental situations and demonstrate the knowledge and the need for sustainable development.
